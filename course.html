Our Bee Keeping Course

The Queen Bee Cafe doesn't just specialise in the cafe line of business but also work with Bee's and Bee Keepers. Our main aim is to use the natural product of honey produced by our Bee's. As a company we would love for our customers to have a chance to experience what it's like being a Bee Keeper and how Bee Keeping works.

In the Bee Keeping world it take's a lot of work to maintain the production from the Bee hives. In this course you will be told about the background of Bee Keeping along with key tips and skills for yourself if you ever would like to delve into the Bee Keeping world and have one of your own Bee hives.
