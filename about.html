About Us

The Queen Bee Café is a company based in the heart of the Scottish Borders, located in the rural town of Melrose. The company itself sell's a range of products such as Bee food products, household products and also a selection of homeware and clothing.

 

Queen Bee Café products are all naturally sourced and produced in the Scottish Borders. The benefit of having the honey bee's in the Borders is they supply the main raw ingredient for the products. Queen Bee also provide Bee Keeping Course's which are organised each month for small groups. On the Bee Keeping course it gives people the full insight into how we gather all of our raw ingredients for our product's and what all happens in the production process.
